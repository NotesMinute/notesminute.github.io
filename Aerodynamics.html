Fluid

Steady Flow
The Parameters of Fluid particles doesnt change with time

Unsteady Flow
The Parameters of Fluid particles change with time

Compressible Flow
Change in density of Fluid is Negligeble

Incompressible Flow
Change in Density of Fluid is Considerable

Uniform Flow
Velocity of Fluid Flow is Constant

Non-Uniform Flow
Velocity of Fluid Flow is not Constant

Rotational Flow
Fluid Particles Rotate about their own Axis

Irrotational Flow
Fluid Particles doesnt rotate about their own axis

Laminar Flow
Have Defnite Path and Doesnt Cross Each other

Turbulant Flow
Doesnt have definite path and they Cross Each other

Ideal Flow
Flow doesnt have any Energy Loss

Real Flow
Energy Loss Occurs due to Friction and heat Transfer

Viscous Flow
Flow in which the viscosity is considerable

In Viscous Flow

Newtonian Flow
If Fluid particles of flow obeys Newtons law of Viscosity is known as Newtonian Flow

Non Newtonian Flow

2-D Flow

3-D Flow
