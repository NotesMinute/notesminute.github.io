Touch Screen

Electrically generated display with input control

Mohammed Rasik

Four principle
Scanning infrared (IR) , Resistive Screen overlay ~ Capacitance overlay ~surface acoustic waves

Scanning infrared
The Infrared (IR) touch panel is one complete unit package integrated with the infrared sensing panel and the controller.

Resistive overlay
Glass substrate over the display screen . Touching the surface will alter resistance

Capacitance overlay
Touching the surface will alter the capacitance and the location is defined

Surface acoustic waves
The change in energy is measured and location defined

Characteristics
Resolution , Speed of responce ~ Image quality ~ Environmental resistance ~ Reliability

Resolution
Discriminate without errors - Coarse (256x256) and fine (4096x4096) resolutions-Depends on the size of the display

Speed of responce
Capacitance overlay is slower than all the other operations

Image quality
Surface acoustic has high transmissivity than all - The glass substrate can reduce light transmitted up to 50 percent

Environmental resistance
Capacitance system may be affected by moisture - IR system can withstand temperature

Reliability
"IR can operate over a large range of shock
