Gas turbine engine inspection and checks

Gas turbine engine inspection and checks consist of 3 basics inspections

Types of Inspections and checks:

1.Visual 2.Structural 3.Dimensional

1.Visual Inspection

1.The first inspection must be done. 2.Basic overview by visual to detect any defect. 3.3 parts that usually examined carefully

The 3 parts:

1. Interior surface of piston pins 2. The fillets at the edges of main crankshaft and crankpin journal surfaces 3. thrust bearing races Includes; cylinder head,piston, valve train, and piston pin

consists of 2 process

1. Degreasing to remove dirt and sludge (soft carbon). 2. The removal of hard carbon deposits by decarbonizing brushing or scraping, and grit-blasting.

There are 5 common structural inspections

1. Dye Penetrant Inspection 2. Eddy Current Inspection 3. Ultrasonic Inspection 4. Magnetic Particle Inspection 5. X-ray

1.Dye Penetrant Inspection

1. Thorough cleaning of the metal surface. 2. Applying penetrant. 3. Removing penetrant with remover emulsifier or cleaner. 4. Drying the part. 5. Applying the developer. 6. Inspecting and interpreting results.

2.Eddy Current Inspection

1. composed of free electrons under the influence of an induced electromagnetic field, that are made to drift through metal. 2. A difference in readings indicates a difference in the hardness state of the affected area.

3.Ultrasonic Inspection

There are 3 basic methods 1. Pulse-Echo 2. Transmission 3. Resonance

1. Pulse-Echo

Measuring the amplitude of signals reflected and the time required for these signals to travel

2. Transmission

Uses two transducers, one to generate the pulse and another placed on the opposite surface to receive it - disruption in the sound path indicates a flaw

4.Magnetic Particle Inspection

Detecting invisible cracks and other defects in ferromagnetic materials

1. magnetizing the part 2. Apply ferromagnetic particles to the surface 3. If a discontinuity is present, the magnetic lines of force are disturbed to assure that the engine’s component parts and clearances meet the manufacturer’s specifications.
