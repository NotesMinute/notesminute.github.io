Systems maintenance procedures of jet engines

Maintenance is any one or combination of activities such as 1.Inspection 2.Modification 3.Repair 4.Replacement 5.Overhaul

Authorities involved in maintenance program development:

1.Regulatory bodies 2.Manufactures 3.Operators

Maintenance regulations and documents:

1.Maintenance manuals 2.Service bulletins 3.Joint Aviation Requirements 4.Federal Aviation Requirements 5.Advisory circulars

Maintenance process:

1.Preventive maintenance 2.Corrective maintenance

1.Preventive maintenance:

It is performed in order to prevent failure of an item to discover a hidden failure

2.Corrective maintenance:

It is performed after the failure to correct the fault.

Hard time maintenance:

A primary maintenance process under which an item must be removed from service at or before a scheduled specified time.

On-condition maintenance:

Inspection/functional check that determines an item's performance and may result in the removal of an item before it fails in service

Conditioning monitoring:

Locating and resolving problem areas through analytical study of malfunctions or failures,not affecting safety of aircraft.
