William Shakespeare Sonnet 18

sonnet 18 is memorable for the skillful and varied presentation of subject matter .It is one of the best known of Shakespeare's sonnets.

comparison of lady love to a Summer's day day:

sonnet 18 begins by comparing the narrator's beloved to summer day using phrases like ," more lovely and more temporary" to describe her beauty and temperament.

superiority of the lady love:

In the sonnet , the speaker argues that his beloved is better than summer season,he also States that his beloved will live forever through his word of poem.

The subject of the poem:

The subject of the poems immortalized in Shakespeare's sonnet , granting them eternal life, beauty, and youth that summer does not have.
