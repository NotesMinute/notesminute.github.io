Key Management in Cryptography

key management refers to managing cryptographic keys with Cryptosystem.

Key management

it deals with generating, exchanging, storing and replacing keys as needed at the user leval.

Process

key management is the process of administration and Managing cryptographic keys for a cryptosystem.

Uses of key management

It is used to administer the full lifecycle of cryptographic keys and protect them from misuse.

Types of keys

Symmetric, asymmetric, private and public.

KEYMANAGEMENT policy

It is a high- leval set of rules that are established by an organization to describe the goals , responsibility for overall key management of cryptographic key material used to protect private , process or information .

Components of key management

Key exchange, key storage, key use, key management system , standard - based key management .l, etc...
