Sand casting,die casting ,centrifugal casting,investment casting,shell molding

Casting is a process in which a material such as metal or plastic in liquid form is poured into a mold and allowed to become hard, in order to make parts or products.

sand casting:

Sand casting is a metal casting process characterized by using sand as the mold material.

Types of sand used in sand casting:

1.Green sand and dry sand, 2.Green sand consists of silica sand, clay, moisture and other additives. Dry sand is a mixture of sand and fast curing adhesive.

Process of sand casting:

There are six steps in this process: 1.Place a pattern in sand to create a mold. 2.Incorporate the pattern and sand in a gating system. 3.Remove the pattern. 4.Fill the mold cavity with molten metal. 5.Allow the metal to cool. 6.Break away the sand mold and remove the casting.

Die casting:

Die casting is a metal casting process that is characterized by forcing molten metal under high pressure into a mould cavity

Types of Die Casting Processes:

1.Low-pressure die casting. 2.Vacuum die casting. 3.Squeeze die casting. 4.Semi-solid die casting.

Uses of die casting:

Accurately dimensioned, sharply defined, smooth or textured-surface metal parts.

Types of die:

Blanking dies , perforating dies , notching dies , trimming , shaving and nibbling dies...etc.

Centrifugal Casting:

Casting that utilizes centrifugal force within a spinning mold to force the metal against the walls is known as Centrifugal Casting

Steps Involved in Centrifugal Casting:

1. Preheat 2. Melt the metal in the furnace. 3. Rotate the mold , pour the metal, and spin the mold until the casting solidifies. 4. Remove the casting from the mold after solidification. 5. Clean, heat-treat and machine the finished casting

Applications of centrifugal casting:

Pipes for water gas sewage, Bearing Bushes, Cylinder Liners, Piston rings ,Paper making rollers, Clutch Plates, Pulleys.

Types of Centrifugal Casting:

1.True Centrifugal Casting, 2.Semi-Centrifugal Casting, 3.Centrifuging.

Investment casting:

Investment casting is a casting process in which a mold is made around a wax pattern that is burned away when the molten material is poured in.

Process of Investment casting:

1. Pattern Making 2. Assembly 3. Shell Building 4. Dewax 5. Metal Casting Pouring 6. Knockout and Cut Off

Applications of investment casting:

Applications of investment casting:

1.Aerospace Industry 2.Medical Instruments 3.Military Equipment 4.3D Printing 5.Automotive Industry...etc

Shell Moulding:

Shell moulding, also known as shell-mould casting, is an expendable mould casting process that uses a resin covered sand to form the mould

Process of shell moulding:

1.Pattern creation 2.Mould creation 3.Mould assembly 4.Pouring 5.cooling 6.Casting removal

Advantages of Shell Mold Casting(Shell Molding):

1.Thin sections, complex parts and intricate designs can be cast 2.Excellent surface finish and goal size tolerances 3.Less machining required for the castings 4.Near net shape castings, almost 'as cast' quality 5.Simplified process that can be handled by semi skilled operators 6.Full mechanized and automated casting process 7.Less foundry space required.
