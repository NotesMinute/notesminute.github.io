Ejector and variable area nozzle of jet engine

Variable area nozzles are used for adjusting the exit area for different operating conditions of the engine

Three Types of variable area nozzles

Central plug at nozzle outlet,Ejector type,Iris Nozzle

Central plug at nozzle outlet

The central plug is very similar to the spike of an intake.Unlike intake,The central plug plug causes external expansion fans.

Ejector type nozzle

Creates an effective nozzle through a secondary air flow .At subsonic speeds,the airflow constricts the exhaust to a convergent shape .As the speed increases,the two nozzles dilate and the two nozzle form a CD Shape.Some configurations may also have a tertiary airflow

Iris nozzle

More complicated than the ejector type nozzle.Offers significantly higher performance.Used in advanced military aircraft.Some of the modern aircraft also have iris nozzles that can be deflected to achieve vectored thrust
