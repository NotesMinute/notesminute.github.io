Principle,Merits,Demerits,Types and application of Extrusion and spinning

Extrusion is a metal forming process in which metal or work piece is forced to flow through a die to reduce its cross section or convert it into desire shape

Merits of Extrusion:

1.Low cost per part. 2.Flexibility of operation. 3.Good mixing (Compounding) 4.Surface finish obtained is good.

Demerits of Extrusion:

1.Variations in size of product. 2.Product limitations 3.High initial cost setup.

Types of Extrusion:

1.Cold Extrusion. 2.Hot Extrusion. 3.Friction Extrusion. 4.Micro Extrusion.

Principle of Extrusion;

1.Simple compressive metal forming process. 2.Piston or plunger is used to apply compressive force at work piece 3.This high compressive force allow the work metal to flow through die and convert into desire shape.

Application of extrusion:

1.production of tubes and hollow pipes. 2.Aluminum extrusion is used in structure work in many industries. 3.Used to produce frames, doors, window etc. in automotive industries. 4.produce plastic objects.

Spinning:

Metal spinning is a method of forming rotationally symmetrical sheet metal parts.

Application of spinning:

1.Vases, baskets, basins and bowls. 2.Bottoms for tanks, hoppers and kettles. 3.Housings for blowers, fans, filters and flywheels. 4.Ladles, nozzles, orifices and tank outlets. 5.Cones, cover s and cups. 6.Funnels, cylinder s, domes and drums. 7.Rings, hemispheres and shells. 8.Vents, venturis and fan wheels.

Principle of spinning:

1.Sheet metal forming process 2.Metal blank is pressed over a rotating chuck to obtain axisymmetric hollow shell

Merits of Spinning:

1.Economically Efficient 2.Cost-Efficient 3.Flexibility 4.Short Lead Times

Demerits of Spinning:

1.Size and Shape Limitations. 2.Production Volume Constraints. 3.Small Allowance for Errors. 4.Manual Spinning Limitations

Types of Spinning:

Ring Spinning, Rotor Spinning, Friction Spinning, Self Twist Spinning, Electro-Static Spinning, Vortex Spinning, Air Jet Spinning, Twist Less Spinning.
